AR Markers 2 Go

AR2Go is a web-based/mobile-friendly display for augmented reality markers compatible with projects like ARToolKit.

This site is an alternative to printing out augmented reality tracking markers. It is also a good option for low-light situations.

Note: The marker display is not yet optimized for horizontal layouts (like desktops and phones in landscape position). In the short term, please lock your device in portrait orientation or use a narrow browser window.
